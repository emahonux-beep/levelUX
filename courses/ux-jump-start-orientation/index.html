Module 1 · Lesson 1 of 4

The people and the mode of work you're in.

schedule 2 min read

This module covers the two things that trip people up most in their first few weeks: not knowing who to go to for what, and not knowing what kind of work they're supposed to be doing. Both are fixable with a little context.

Work through the lessons in order if you're brand new, or jump straight to whatever you need. You can check off each lesson as you go — your progress saves automatically.

info How this module is organized

We start with the people (who's who and how to approach them), then move into the mode of work (discovery vs. delivery) — because knowing which one you're in changes everything about how you show up.
